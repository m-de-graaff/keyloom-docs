If you enable the JWT session strategy, validate these settings.

<Steps>

### 1) Issuer

Set `jwt.issuer` and ensure it matches the audience's expectation.

### 2) AUTH_SECRET

Must be base64url and decode to at least 32 bytes. Use `keyloom doctor --fix` to generate.

### 3) TTLs and skew

Adjust `jwt.accessTTL`, `jwt.refreshTTL`, and `jwt.clockSkewSec` to match your environment.

### 4) Key rotation

Use `jwt.keyRotationDays` and `jwt.keyOverlapDays` to avoid invalidating active sessions when rotating keys.

</Steps>

<Callout>
Prefer database sessions unless you specifically need JWT sessions across services or non-browser clients.
</Callout>
